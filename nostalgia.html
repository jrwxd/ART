Digital Whispers

pixels scatter like dust
in the virtual wind
ones and zeros dancing
an electric ballet

we are ghosts in the machine
memories stored on magnetic tape
our whispers echo through 
fluorescent corridors of light

the internet remembers
when we have forgotten
cached versions of ourselves
forever loading...
        
connection lost.
reconnecting...
