Meet Gregory Jones

Inspired by Nature

Driven by a lifelong passion for the stark, captivating beauty of Oklahoma and the American West, Gregory Jones established Black Mesa Sky. It's more than just photography; it's about sharing unique perspectives, often from the air, that reveal the soul of the landscape.

Named after the iconic Black Mesa, our work strives to capture the timeless spirit, dramatic light, and natural wonder of this region. We specialize in high-quality landscape, aerial, and potentially wildlife photography [**NEEDS INFO**], offering both fine art prints and commissioned projects.
